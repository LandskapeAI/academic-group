{{ "BIASlab is an academic research team at Eindhoven University of Technology. Our research focuses on nature-inspired artificial intelligence technology for engineering applications. We are inspired by recent results in computational neuroscience (the [Free Energy Principle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_energy_principle)) on how the brain perceives, learns and makes decisions through unsupervised real-time interactions with its environment. \n\n Our mission is to translate these ideas to *synthetic agents* that automate the design of signal processing and control systems through real-time unsupervised interactions with its environment. Applications range from real-time design of multimedia applications (e.g., personalized hearing aid algorithms) to decision making for robot and drone navigation." | markdownify }}
